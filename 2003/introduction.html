Workshop Introduction

The 2nd European NKOS Workshop took place on 21st August, as part of ECDL 2003 in Trondheim. The Workshop was attended by 35 people. It was based around the theme of emerging standards in Networked Knowledge Organization Systems and Services. The final Workshop programme contains links to slides of the presentations. A report of the workshop appeared in the September 2003 issue of D-Lib. A special issue on New Applications Of Knowledge Organisation Systems, which built on the workshop was published in the Journal of Digital Information, 4(4).
