Workshop Introduction

The objective of the NKOS Workshop is to facilitate the exchange of viewpoints and ideas about topics and problems related to user-centred development and design of NKOS. The issues discussed may range from considerations related to the role of KOS, conceptual content and design, methodological issues, and technical issues (standards, protocols and XML-RDF technologies).
