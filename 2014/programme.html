Workshop Schedule

The 13th European NKOS workshop, co-organized with ISKO UK, took place on the afternoon of Thursday 11th September and the morning of Friday 12th September as part of DL2014 (joint JCDL/TPDL conferences) in City University,London. The NKOS workshop was held in A227 in the College Building (off St. John Street)

NOTE: Following the workshop a call for papers has been issued for a special issue on NKOS for the International Journal on Digital Libraries
