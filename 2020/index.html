20th European Networked Knowledge Organization Systems (NKOS) Workshop

The organisers regret to inform you that unfortunately due to the current and ongoing Covid-19 / coronavirus situation and associated restrictions the 20th European Networked Knowledge Organization Systems (NKOS) workshop which was to have been held as part of TPDL 2020 in Lyon, France on Tuesday 25th August 2020 has now been cancelled.
